I'm a technology enthusiast passionate about the systems that connect us. My interests are focused on cloud infrastructure, virtualization, and radio communication.

Professionally, I'm leveling up my skills in cloud computing and am always studying for new certifications. I'm excited to master cloud architecture to build and manage modern, scalable, and efficient solutions.

At home, I get hands-on experience running my own home lab. I use it to experiment with self-hosting and test virtualization concepts in a practical environment. This 'tinkerer's mindset' helps me bridge theory and real-world application.

When I'm not in front of a server, I'm on the air as an amateur radio operator. This hobby combines my love for electronics with the magic of global communication.

I'm driven by a desire to understand systems from the ground up—from the physical layer to the cloud—and am always looking to learn and connect with others.
